个人单机游戏隐私政策

Privacy Policy for Single-player Games

一、总则

1. General Provisions

本隐私政策旨在说明本人所开发的单机游戏（以下简称“本游戏”）在用户使用过程中的信息收集与处理相关事宜。

本人承诺，所开发的单机游戏均为纯单机模式，不会以任何形式收集、存储、使用或分享用户的个人信息，包括但不限于姓名、身份证号、手机号、位置信息、设备标识符、使用行为数据等。

This Privacy Policy is intended to explain matters related to the collection and processing of user information during the use of the single-player games (hereinafter referred to as "the Games") developed by me.

I hereby commit that all the single-player games developed are purely offline mode and will not collect, store, use or share user's personal information in any form, including but not limited to name, ID number, phone number, location information, device identifier, usage behavior data, etc.

二、信息收集说明

2. Information Collection

本游戏在运行过程中，不会主动请求获取用户的任何权限（如位置权限、通讯录权限、相机权限等），也不会通过任何技术手段收集用户的个人信息或设备信息。

所有游戏数据（如存档、设置等）均仅存储在用户的本地设备中，不会上传至任何服务器。

During the operation of the Games, no permissions (such as location, contacts, camera, etc.) will be actively requested from users, nor will any technical means be used to collect users' personal or device information.

All game data (such as save files, settings, etc.) are stored only on the user's local device and will not be uploaded to any server.
